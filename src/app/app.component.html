About
Merck Academy presentations are live, in-person lectures, facilitated by Merck subject-matter experts. All of the Merck Academy faculty have postdoctoral training and are engaged in a variety of medical and scientific activities.
These live presentations comprise a variety of general scientific topics, including drug discovery, vaccine development, outcomes research, clinical trials, and statistics. Additional topics continue to be developed and added.
Potential audiences for Merck Academy might include faculty, fellows, residents, and medical students. Merck Academy is not intended to provide continuing medical education (CME) credits.
To learn more about Merck Academy, the topics covered, or to schedule a Merck speaker, please contact a Merck account team member.
